About Me

I recently graduated from the University of Mary Hardin-Baylor with a degree in Multimedia and Information Technology and a minor in art. It was there that I first got interested in User Experience. I loved the idea of combining my passion for design, with psychology and coding. It was like my three favorite things rolled into one career! From there I decided I needed more experience and so I applied to a UX/UI Bootcamp through UT Austin and just graduated from that this December. I have been loving every minute of it and can't wait to start using my skills to actually make a difference. I love photography, movies, and anything that lets my creative side shine. I am a native Austinite who loves traveling and have spent several months living overseas.
